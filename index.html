Init is a non-profit technology conference focused on developing a thriving software development community in Banja Luka and the region.

Organized by developers for developers, Init is your chance to spend a day upgrading your skills and connecting with some incredibly talented people.

Why attend
Init 2022

Init 2022 is a unique opportunity to hear directly from 17 prominent speakers from the development community and hear their thoughts on the current state and the future of software development.

With two speaker tracks and one workshop track devoted to both veterans and software industry newbies, Init 2022 caters to all members of the community, bringing them closer together for a day of learning and skill building.

Connect

Meet the software industry professionals from the city and beyond. Get connected, find jobs, or start new projects. Init is primarily about you. Get yourself connected, find jobs, or start new projects.

Learn

Hear stories from industry veterans who have been there and done that. Improve your problem-solving skills, get a new sense of direction for your career and upgrade your thinking.

Give back

Init is non-profit and completely funded by donations. Buying a ticket is your way of showing support for our small but brave development community, and one little step towards building a brighter future.

1 DAY

We pack a ton of valuable content into a single day, which makes INIT perfect if you are on a busy schedule.

20 SPEAKERS

Two speaker tracks, hand-picked content for both veterans and newbies. Whatever you are into, INIT offers an exceptional learning experience.

Venue

Banski Dvor is one of the most beautiful historical buildings, located in the very heart of the city it was built in the period 1931-32 as the seat of Duke of Vrbas Banovina. The building is close to all major hotels and city attractions, with plenty of restaurants around. Parking spots are available on several nearby locations.
